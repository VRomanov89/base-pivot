Common Questions

Do you provide real estate agent services?

No, we are not real estate agents. We are an independent research platform focused on providing market insights, neighborhood intelligence, and decision-making tools. We do not buy, sell, or list properties.

Can you help me find a specific property?

We don't help with property searches, but we can provide context about neighborhoods, market conditions, and decision frameworks that will help you evaluate properties you're considering.

Do you offer personalized consulting?

We offer limited personalized consulting for people actively making real estate decisions in Montreal. Contact us to discuss your situation and we'll let you know if we can help.

How much does Base Pivot cost?

Our newsletter, insights, neighborhood profiles, and tools are completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core content will always remain accessible.

Can I use your data for my own project?

We're open to partnerships and data sharing for legitimate research or educational purposes. Please reach out to discuss your specific needs and we'll see if we can help.

How can I support Base Pivot?

The best way to support us is to share our content with people who would find it useful and subscribe to our newsletter. We also welcome feedback and suggestions for new features or content.
